ABOUT PUBLIC HEALTH – UNIMAK

The Public Health BSc (Hons) program at the University of Makeni is a four-year degree program that includes coursework, dissertation writing, and an internship.

The program is structured into two major areas. In the second year, students have the opportunity to specialize in:

  • Public Health Epidemiology
  • Public Health Laboratory Sciences

This specialization equips students with essential skills and knowledge relevant to both epidemiology and the public health laboratory sector.
